About Jennifer

My name is Chukwuma Kosiso Jennifer. I am a Front-End Developer currently on a journey to master the art of creating beautiful, functional, and user-friendly websites. With a foundation in HTML, CSS, Bootstrap and growing skills in Javascript, I am building my portfolio one project at a time.

MY JOURNEY INTO TECH

Unlike most people, I didn’t always know I wanted to be a developer. My journey into tech began unexpectedly, i had a conversation with a mentor and it sparked my within me. I started my research and fell in-love with Front-End Development. I watched any and every tutorial on youtube to learn. To solidify my skills, I enrolled in a short-term front-end development program at Genesys Tech hub and later graduated from another short program at Pluralcode Academy, where I honed my knowledge. These experiences equipped me with the tools to create functional and visually appealing websites. Today, I’m on a continuous journey of learning and growth, working on real-world projects that showcase my creativity and passion for web development. I am currently enrolled as a Front-End Engineering Student at AltSchool Africa learning the foundations of Front-End development.

When i am not coding, I am probably catching up on anime, reading manhwa or blasting my ears off with loud alternative pop.
